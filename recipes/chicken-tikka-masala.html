Chicken Tikka Masala

A creamy and flavorful Indian dish with tender chicken in a rich tomato gravy. Perfect served with rice or naan!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • Cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Directions

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a large bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, ginger, turmeric, salt, and pepper. Add chicken and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the chicken: Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned on all sides. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. Make the sauce: In the same pan, add onion and cook until softened. Add garlic and ginger paste, and cook for another minute. Stir in crushed tomatoes, chicken broth, and bring to a simmer.
  4. Return the chicken to the pan and simmer for 20 minutes, or until cooked through.
  5. Stir in heavy cream and simmer for another 5 minutes. Season with additional salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
